Tech-Refresh Brand Identity
A complete brand guide for the interview prep and hiring pipeline tool. Designed for developers leveling up, job hunters, and students facing the "painful period" of job search — serious purpose with playful, encouraging energy.

Brand Positioning
Promise: Navigate the job hunt with confidence, one skill at a time.

Archetype: The Mentor + Cheerleader (serious expertise + genuine encouragement)

Audience:

Developers leveling up to the next role
Active job hunters (week 1 → week 52)
CS students preparing for careers
Tone:

Primary: Calm, clear, competent (like Linear, Supabase)
Secondary: Playful morale-booster during hard moments (mascot celebrations, XP wins, rank-ups)
Never: Patronizing, frivolous, or tone-deaf to job search stress
Visual Identity
Core Aesthetic
Inspired by Linear (minimal, geometric grace) and Supabase (dark, accessible, developer-first).

Palette: Dark elevation ladder + teal accent (existing tokens.js)
Style: Organic, rounded forms (not cold geometric)
Hierarchy: Calm surfaces, teal highlights, dark breathing room
Motion: Intentional, celebratory (Reanimated on mobile)
Logo
Concept: Organic upward motion + teal + literary depth

Visual Language:

Flowing, hand-drawn quality (like Supabase's wavy form) but more structured
Upward trajectory or ascending path
Teal as primary color on dark background
Readable at 16px (favicon) through 512px (social)
Works in monochrome (for accessibility, print, small sizes)
Logo Variations:

Primary mark: Full logo with wordmark (horizontal lockup)
Symbol only: Standalone icon for app, favicon, avatar
Stacked: Logo above/below wordmark (vertical lockup for app stores)
Monochrome: Black/white + teal (social, press, accessibility)
Design Direction: Imagine a path or flow that ascends organically, like a bird's journey or a career trajectory. Teal highlights the "forward momentum." The form should feel alive, not rigid.

Mascot: The Raven
Name: Quill (or Raven, or Poe — your choice)

Inspiration: Edgar Allan Poe's "The Raven" — wisdom, mystery, resilience, literary depth. A crow/raven that's clever, encouraging, and slightly mischievous.

Personality:

Wise but not condescending
Celebratory (pops up during XP wins, rank-ups, quiz perfect scores)
Sardonic sense of humor (acknowledges job search pain)
Supportive cheerleader ("You got this" energy)
Visual Design:

Organic, slightly illustrated style (not cartoonish, not photo-real)
Intelligent eyes (reflecting thoughtfulness)
Teal accent (feathers, eye glint, or aura)
Size: Works as a small corner element (mobile notification) to full celebration burst
Animations: Landing, bobbing, wing flutter during celebrations
Key Moments:

First rank-up: Quill lands on screen, does a little celebration dance
Quiz perfect score: Quill flies across the screen with confetti
Job search milestone (7-day streak, etc.): Quill delivers an encouraging message
Error states: Quill tilts sympathetically ("That didn't work, but you've got this")
Tone in Text: When Quill "speaks," use phrases like:

"One skill at a time, friend."
"Your next role is in there somewhere."
"Back to the practice board."
"This is the way." (Poe + Mandalorian energy)

Logo Execution Roadmap
Phase 1: Concept (Midjourney, 2–3 hours)
Prompt for Midjourney:

A minimal, organic logo combining teal (#14B8A6) and dark background.
Symbolizes: career growth journey, upward momentum, bird flight, resilience.
Style: Modern, flowing, hand-drawn quality like Supabase but more organic curves.
Inspired by: Linear's minimalism, Supabase's fluidity.
Elements: Ascending path or flowing form suggesting forward motion. Teal as primary.
Never: Hard geometric shapes, corporate clip-art, too many details.
Context: For developers, job hunters, students. Tech-interview prep + hiring tools.
Usage: App icon, favicon, social media, website header.
Format: SVG-ready (clean vector lines, minimal fills).
Expected output: 4–5 variations to choose from.

Once you pick a direction from Midjourney:

Create lockup variations:
Horizontal (logo + wordmark)
Stacked (vertical)
Symbol only (favicon)
Monochrome (black, white, teal)
Test at multiple sizes (16px, 32px, 64px, 256px)
Phase 3: Mascot (Midjourney + Figma, 2–3 hours)
Prompt for Midjourney (Raven/Quill):

A clever, wise raven or crow in an organic, slightly illustrated style.
Personality: Intelligent, encouraging, slightly mischievous. Companion mascot.
Visual style: Not cartoonish, not photo-real. Organic flowing lines like our brand.
Colors: Black with teal accents (eye glint, feather highlights). Warm gold (#D4A574) for depth.
Size variations: Works as small corner element (20px) to full celebration burst (400px).
Emotion: Can convey wisdom, celebration, sympathy, encouragement through posture/expression.
Inspired by: Edgar Allan Poe's "The Raven" + modern minimalism.
Context: Appears during job hunt milestones, quiz wins, rank-ups, error states.
Format: Illustrated, animation-friendly (layered for Lottie or frame-based animation).
Expected output: 3–4 character variations (different poses, emotions).

Tone of Voice
When writing copy with the Raven mascot or brand voice:

Empathetic Language:

❌ "Failed quiz" → ✅ "Let's build on this"
❌ "No jobs yet" → ✅ "Your next role is out there"
❌ "Only 3/10" → ✅ "3 stronger than yesterday"
Encouraging but Honest:

"This is hard. You're doing it anyway. That counts."
"One skill at a time, friend. That's how mastery works."
"Rejected today, stronger tomorrow."
Playful but Respectful:

Use Poe references sparingly ("Nevermore struggle" on a win, "Quoth the Raven: try again" on retry)
Celebrate wins, acknowledge pain (never trivialize job search)
Light humor, never mean-spirited
